Stay Safe Online – Checklist

Prevention Tips
Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) wherever possible
Avoid clicking on unknown links or email attachments
Download apps and software only from trusted sources
Keep your device updated with latest security patches
Never share OTP, PIN, or passwords with anyone (UPI Safety)
Always verify UPI payment requests before approving (UPI Safety)
Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or sensitive transactions
Regularly backup important data to prevent loss
Log out from accounts on shared or public devices
Report Cybercrime:
If you face any cyber fraud or suspicious activity:
 National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
 Helpline Number: 1930

Objective
To promote safe and responsible internet usage among college students in India and reduce the risk of cyber threats.
