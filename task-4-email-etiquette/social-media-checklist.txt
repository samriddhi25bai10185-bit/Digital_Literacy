Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

Social Media Do’s

Think before you post or comment.
Use privacy settings to control who sees your posts.
Verify facts before sharing news or information.
Be respectful and professional in communication.
Report harmful content or cyberbullying.

Social Media Don’ts

Don’t share personal information publicly.
Don’t engage in online arguments or fights.
Don’t post offensive or inappropriate content.
Don’t overshare your daily activities constantly.
Don’t use social media during classes or important study time
